Who we are?

We are a bunch of sports lovers who tries to keep in shape in a more and more fast moving world! We are looking to create a unic environment for the ones which love sports like us, but don't have enough time to organise a friends meeting with the porpoise of playing a Football, Tennis, Basket match or any other sport available around them. This application will serve as a quick and easy way to attend an already organised match with other players which use the same app.

  • You can book a field and add users!
  • You can join a field and play with strangers!
  • You can check what fields and games are playing in your city!
